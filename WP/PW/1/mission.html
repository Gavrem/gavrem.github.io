Our Mission

There's a world of choice in today's car market, what with more than 400 different models and many car types available. Making sense of it all can be difficult. What are the different types of cars? The first thing to know is that "cars" means "passenger vehicles of all types." For the purposes of keeping it simple, we've broken down all of those vehicle choices into three major categories covering all of the basic types of vehicles from coupes to full-size sedans to crossover SUVs.

Our mission is to create an platform where people can find information about type of cars so that they can dedice which is suit thier needs before buying.
